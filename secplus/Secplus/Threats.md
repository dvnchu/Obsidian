## Social engineering attacks

* Phishing
	Attackers try to convince you they are someone else and make you give up information
	- Check address
	- check links (typosquatting)
	- pretexts
	diff baits ->
	- Vishing(voice)
	- smishing (SMS)

* **Impersonation**
	They make up a story
	They convince you they are someone using known details that you recognize*
	They can extract information and you dont realize
	They  use well documented psychological tricks
	
	credit card/bank/loan/gov benefits fraud
	
	- never volunteer information
	- dont disclose personal details
	- always verify before revealing info
	- verification should be encouraged

* **Watering hole attack**
	Attackers cant get in so they poison some resource you will use before you get there
	
	Determine which website the victim group uses
	
	can infect "water" for all users
	
	How to stop it?
	Defense in depth
	Firewalls IPS
	anti virus

* **Misinformation**
Disseminate factually incorrect info
Influence campaigns advertising social media

- **Brand impersonation**
	Create tens of thousands of impersonated sites to see if one succeeds
	Malware is almost guaranteed
